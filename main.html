Summary

Inception follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who specializes in entering people's dreams to steal secrets. He is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform "inception"—planting an idea into someone's mind. The journey dives deep into layered dreams, reality, and the power of the subconscious.

Main Actors

Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, Joseph Gordon-Levitt as Arthur, Ellen Page (Emma Thomas) as Ariadne, and Tom Hardy as Eames.
